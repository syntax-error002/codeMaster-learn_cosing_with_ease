What's the best way to use the practice problems?

• Start with Easy problems to build confidence
• Move to Medium problems (most common in interviews)
• Time yourself (30-45 minutes per problem)
• Review solutions after attempting
Pro Tip: Focus on understanding patterns rather than memorizing solutions
